## Big 8

Have you heard of the "big 8" aspects of diversity? Including this here for consideration of other questions to include if you want to be "fully" comprehensive.

    2. Age (addressed)
    3. Ethnicity (addressed)

    5. Race (addressed)

    7. Sexual Orientation
